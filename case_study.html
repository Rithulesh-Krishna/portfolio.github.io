About

The following case studies are of different projects where I worked on the design, IA, user research, wireframing and building high-fidelity mockups / prototypes. 2 of these Case studies are for applications that is meant to cater the pre-existing clients of eShipz.

CXO Cockpit

This case study offers a detailed examination of CXO Cockpit, a sophisticated executive decision support platform primarily tailored for CEOs, CFOs, and other top-level executives. With its emphasis on real-time data aggregation, customizable dashboards, and advanced analytics, CXO Cockpit equips executives with the tools needed to swiftly access and analyze crucial information. By enabling informed decision-making, it fosters strategic agility and long-term success in today's dynamic business landscape, empowering organizations to maintain a competitive edge.

MGL - Demand Forecasting Tool

This case study highlights the creation of Mahanagar Gas Limited's (MGL) Demand Forecasting Platform, a comprehensive solution designed to optimize gas distribution operations. The project focused on developing a user-centric platform tailored to meet the specific needs of MGL and its stakeholders. Through meticulous design and implementation, the study delves into the process of crafting an end-to-end solution, from conceptualization to deployment. The platform's architecture, functionalities, and usability principles are examined, showcasing how it empowers MGL to accurately forecast demand, optimize resources, and ensure efficient gas distribution across the Mumbai metropolitan area.

CloudBankin

Cloudbankin is a forward-thinking company specializing in innovative financial technology solutions that enhance operational efficiency and data management. Our latest project focuses on developing a versatile form builder to streamline data collection processes. This platform will enable users to create multi-page forms with diverse field types and customizable layouts, addressing key challenges in data collection and user experience. By offering intuitive design and robust functionality, Cloudbankin's Form Builder aims to provide comprehensive insights and improve decision-making, making it a valuable tool for organizations seeking to optimize their operations and enhance data accuracy.

MyCo Spend Management Platform

This case study showcases the design improvements made to MyCo's employee spend management platform, which allows employees to raise spend requests and managers to respond accordingly. By introducing a new feature that creates a virtual credit card for each spend request, the platform addresses a key issue for the company. The study delves into the user-centered design principles used to develop this feature, and how it improved the overall user experience.

Netflix Information Architecture & Account's flow analyses

This case study focuses on the information architecture and account flow of Netflix, the leading provider of streaming entertainment. The study examines the user-centered design principles used in the creation of Netflix's platform and the ways in which they have optimized the experience for their users. The account flow of Netflix is also analyzed, highlighting the simplicity and efficiency of the process for creating and managing a user account.
