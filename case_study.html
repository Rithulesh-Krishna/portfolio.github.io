Case Studies

About

The following case studies are for 3 different projects where I worked on the design, IA, user research, wireframing and building high-fidelity mockups. 2 of these Case studies are for applications that is meant to cater the pre-existing clients of eShipz. and another one being a case study for Netflix.

Prana Yoga

This case study highlights the user experience improvements made to the course selection process for Prana Yoga. By adding a new page to the user flow, specifically for selecting a yoga instructor, the company was able to address a key pain point for their customers. The study explores the user-centered design principles employed to create this page and how it helped improve the overall user experience.

Netflix Information Architecture & Account's flow analyses

This case study focuses on the information architecture and account flow of Netflix, the leading provider of streaming entertainment. The study examines the user-centered design principles used in the creation of Netflix's platform and the ways in which they have optimized the experience for their users. The account flow of Netflix is also analyzed, highlighting the simplicity and efficiency of the process for creating and managing a user account.
